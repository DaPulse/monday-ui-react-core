## Variants

### Sizes

Sizes small/medium/large are available.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Sizes">
    <Slider size={Slider.sizes.SMALL} valueDefault={12} />
    <Slider size={Slider.sizes.MEDIUM} valueDefault={24} />
    <Slider size={Slider.sizes.LARGE} valueDefault={35} />
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### Colors

Color Modes primary/positive/negative are available.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Colors">
    <Slider color={Slider.colors.POSITIVE} valueDefault={34} size={Slider.sizes.MEDIUM} />
    <Slider color={Slider.colors.NEGATIVE} valueDefault={55} size={Slider.sizes.MEDIUM} />
    <Slider color={Slider.colors.PRIMARY} valueDefault={12} size={Slider.sizes.MEDIUM} />
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### Disabled

Slider can be disabled.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Disabled">
    <Slider disabled={true} valueDefault={24} color={Slider.colors.POSITIVE} size={Slider.sizes.MEDIUM} />
    <Slider disabled={true} color={Slider.colors.NEGATIVE} size={Slider.sizes.MEDIUM} />
    <Slider disabled={true} valueDefault={55} color={Slider.colors.PRIMARY} size={Slider.sizes.MEDIUM} />
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### With labels

Indicate selection at Label, Add Prefix and/or Postfix Icons or Labels

  <Story name="ShowValue">

Custom ID and Custom (Consumer) Classes. Check HTML of component.
Add Custom Items as Prefix and Postfix of Slider.
